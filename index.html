I am currently an PhD Student of Mathematics under the supervise of Hehui Wu at the Fudan University, Shanghai, China. . I graduated with a Bachelor Degree in Mathematics from University of Science and Technology of China in June 2021.

My research interests include Extremal Combinatorics, Digraph and Graph embedding. I have published papers in journals such as the Journal of Combinatorial Theory, Series B. I have been a teaching assistant on linear algebra, graph theory, and abstract algrbra at both undergraduate and graduate levels.
